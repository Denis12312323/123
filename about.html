It is believed that the basis of Ukrainian culinary traditions was laid more than 7 thousand years ago by the people of Trypillia! Then our ancestors learned how to make bread, which became “the head of everything” for a millennium. The phenomenon of Ukrainian cuisine is that it is both whole and has regional diversity.

Since our lands were parts of different states for a long time and were under the influence of different nationalities, the cultural characteristics of the regions were formed separately from each other. For example, sheep products are more common in Western Ukraine, and potato dishes are more common in Polesie.

“ This website was created for whole world could get acquainted with the incredible national cuisine of Ukraine, in all regions. Because only in Ukraine you can try borscht, dumplings, banosh, dumplings, meatballs and many other national treasures of Ukraine. ”
